About Us

Who We Are SafeRide is a women-centric cab booking platform dedicated to providing a safe, reliable, and empowering transportation service exclusively for women. We are committed to building a community where women can travel with confidence, knowing that their safety, comfort, and convenience are our top priorities. Designed by women, for women, SafeRide is more than just a cab service — it’s a movement toward safer, more inclusive travel.

Our Mission Our mission is to create a transportation ecosystem where women feel safe, valued, and empowered. By connecting female passengers with verified female drivers, we aim to foster trust, build community, and redefine what safe travel means for women worldwide. Our Vision To become the most trusted and preferred cab service for women by leveraging technology, safety innovations, and a community-driven approach. We envision a world where women no longer need to compromise on safety when choosing transportation.

Our Values Safety First: Every feature we design is centered around ensuring the highest standards of safety. Empowerment: Providing earning opportunities for women drivers and enhancing independence for women riders. Community and Trust: Building a network of women supporting women in their daily travels. Convenience with Confidence: Seamless booking, real-time tracking, and a stress-free experience.

What Sets Us Apart Women-Only Service: SafeRide is designed exclusively for female drivers and passengers, creating a space where women can travel comfortably and safely. Thoroughly Vetted Drivers: Every driver undergoes a rigorous background check, professional training, and continuous safety evaluations. Advanced Safety Features: SOS Emergency Button Live Location Sharing 24/7 Support Team Empowering Women Drivers: We provide economic opportunities for women to become drivers, promoting financial independence and breaking gender barriers in transportation.

Why We Do It Every woman deserves to feel safe on her journey. Unfortunately, transportation safety concerns disproportionately affect women worldwide. SafeRide was born from the belief that women deserve a safe, secure, and trusted travel option — a service they can rely on, no matter the time or place. Join Us on Our Mission SafeRide isn’t just a service; it’s a community working toward safer mobility for women. Whether you’re a passenger or a driver, you’re part of a movement where we ride together, safely.
